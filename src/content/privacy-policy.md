## 1. Overview & Scope

Vigilant Labs ("we", "us", "our", "Company") operates the CrowdSense™ platform, an AI-powered video analytics service that processes video footage from customer-provided CCTV cameras to deliver real-time operational intelligence. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, disclose, and safeguard personal data and video footage under the **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023**, India's **Factories Act, 1948**, **OSHWC Code 2020**, **FSSAI Regulations**, and applicable international data protection standards.

**This Privacy Policy applies to:**
- Personal data collected from website visitors, demo requesters, and platform users
- Video footage processed through the CrowdSense™ platform
- Usage data and interaction logs generated by customers

**This Privacy Policy does NOT apply to:**
- Video footage or metadata that remains solely under customer control on their premises
- Data processed by our customers in their own systems (customers are independent data controllers)

## 2. Data Categories & Collection Methods

### 2.1 Personal Data Collected Directly From Users

**Website & Inquiry Data:**
- Name, email address, phone number, company name
- Job title, industry sector, organization size
- Camera count, deployment location, use case description
- IP address, browser type, device information

**Collection Methods:**
- Contact forms (demo requests, inquiries)
- Email communications
- Phone calls and meetings
- Live chat interactions

**Legal Basis:** Explicit consent + legitimate interest (marketing, product improvement)

### 2.2 Video Footage & Metadata

**What We Process:**
- Raw or compressed video streams from customer CCTV cameras
- AI-extracted metadata: detected objects (persons, PPE items, safety violations), timestamps, bounding boxes, confidence scores
- Aggregated analytics: shift reports, compliance scores, KPIs per workstation

**Collection Method:**
- Encrypted transmission via secure tunnel (on-premise deployment) or direct cloud connection (cloud deployment)

**Retention Period:**
- Raw video: Deleted automatically based on customer retention policy (typically 7–30 days); not retained by us unless customer explicitly requests archival
- AI detections & alerts: Retained for 90 days (compliant with RBI guidelines for financial audits) unless customer requests extension
- Compliance reports (PDFs, snapshots): Retained for 1 year per regulatory audit requirements

**Legal Basis:** Customer's explicit consent as data controller; compliance with Factories Act 1948, OSHWC Code 2020, FSSAI regulations

### 2.3 Usage & Interaction Data

**Platform Telemetry:**
- Login timestamps, feature usage, queries executed
- Alerts triggered, compliance scores, anomalies detected
- API calls, data export requests, user roles/permissions changed
- Error logs (anonymized)

**Collection Method:** Automated logging via the CrowdSense™ platform

**Retention Period:** 6 months (30 days if customer requests deletion)

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest (security, performance monitoring, abuse prevention)

## 4. Data Security & Encryption

### 4.1 Video & Footage Security

**In Transit:**
- End-to-end encryption via TLS 1.3
- Secure tunnel (on-premise deployment): Outbound-only, encrypted via WireGuard/Wirex
- No inbound firewall ports required

**At Rest:**
- AES-256 encryption for stored video/metadata
- Encrypted databases with key management via AWS KMS / Azure Key Vault
- Separate encryption keys per customer tenant

### 4.2 Personal Data Security

- Encrypted databases (AES-256)
- Hashed passwords (bcrypt with salt)
- API tokens & credentials: Short-lived, rotated every 90 days
- Access logs: Retained for 90 days for audit purposes

### 4.3 Infrastructure & Compliance

- Hosted on **Vercel** (SOC 2 Type II certified) / **AWS** (ISO 27001 certified)
- Data centers in India (Mumbai) for regulatory compliance
- DDoS protection, WAF (Web Application Firewall), rate limiting
- Regular security audits & penetration testing (annual)

### 4.4 Employee Access Controls

- Principle of least privilege (role-based access control)
- Multi-factor authentication required for all staff
- No direct access to customer video footage (abstracted via AI outputs only)
- Access logs monitored & reviewed quarterly

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion

### 5.1 Retention Schedules

**Video Footage:**
- Default: 7–30 days (per customer configuration) — then automatically deleted
- Extended (if requested): Up to 90 days — per RBI/FSSAI audit requirements
- Never stored longer than necessary for stated operational purpose

**AI Detections & Reports:**
- 90 days (per RBI guidelines for financial audit trails)
- Customers can request deletion anytime; we delete within 5 business days

**Personal Data (Contact Info):**
- Account active: Full retention
- Account closed/abandoned: Deleted within 30 days (unless legal hold applies)
- Unsubscribed from marketing: Removed from mailing lists immediately; retained for transactional email only

**Compliance Records (PDFs, Reports):**
- Retained for 1 year (per Factories Act Section 92, FSSAI Regulation 10, ISO 22000:2018 requirements)
- Longer retention only if mandated by law or customer request

### 5.2 Right to Deletion & Erasure

Customers have the right to request deletion of:
- Their personal data (name, email, company info) — deleted within 30 days
- Video footage — deleted immediately (with caveat: may be legally required if a compliance audit is ongoing)
- AI detection logs — deleted within 5 business days

**Limitations to Deletion:**
- Cannot delete if legal proceedings are active
- Cannot delete compliance reports during audit window (up to 1 year)
- Cannot delete data required for tax compliance (7-year retention)

**Request:** Email privacy@vigilantlabs.in with "Data Deletion Request" + account ID

## 8. Children & Sensitive Data

### 8.1 Age Restriction

CrowdSense™ is intended for business/organizational use only. We do not knowingly collect data from persons under 18. If you are under 18, do not use this service.

### 8.2 Sensitive Personal Data

Video footage may incidentally capture:
- Biometric identifiers (faces, gait, PPE worn)
- Health indicators (e.g., person moving slowly may indicate health issue)
- Religious or cultural identifiers (clothing, objects)

**Our Commitment:**
- Video is processed **only for the stated operational purpose** (PPE detection, fire detection, SOP monitoring)
- Biometric data is extracted for safety compliance only; not for surveillance, identification, or profiling
- Facial recognition is **disabled by default** and only enabled if customer explicitly requests (with added consent)
- No dossier-building or long-term behavioral tracking

## 10. Your Rights Under Indian Law

### 10.1 Under DPDP Act 2023

You have the right to:

1. **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data — we provide within 30 days
2. **Correction:** Request updates to inaccurate or incomplete data — we correct within 15 days
3. **Deletion (Right to be Forgotten):** Request erasure (except where legal hold applies) — we delete within 30 days
4. **Data Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format (CSV, JSON) — we provide within 15 days
5. **Withdraw Consent:** Stop processing at any time — opt-out via email; email communications cease within 7 days
6. **Grievance Redressal:** File a complaint with us or with the Data Protection Board of India (DPBI)

**To Exercise Rights:** Email privacy@vigilantlabs.in with "DPDP Request — [Type]" + customer ID

### 10.2 Under Factories Act 1948 & OSHWC Code 2020

If CrowdSense™ is used for workplace safety monitoring:
- You have the right to inspect compliance records (with management approval)
- Footage is retained only for safety/audit purposes (not for worker discipline/punishment)
- Workers are notified of CCTV via signage
- No covert filming in private areas (restrooms, medical rooms, prayer rooms)

### 10.3 Under FSSAI Regulations (Food Manufacturing)

If used in food manufacturing:
- Footage is part of your facility's HACCP records and compliance documentation
- You retain ownership of all footage and compliance data
- We merely provide the processing service
